Marcus walked into the trendy diner on the corner and ordered a sparkling soda, expecting its effervescence to perk him up. The waiter promptly delivered the chilled drink. Before Marcus could even lift the glass, his phone buzzed with a reminder for a meeting in the diner’s adjoining conference room—starting in five minutes. He quickly left the counter and stepped into the room next door. As he settled in, he found himself missing that sparkling beverage.
