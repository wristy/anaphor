Elena walked into the quaint patisserie nestled beside her office building, eager for a quick treat. She ordered a fluffy croissant, and the cheerful baker promptly placed it on the counter.
Before Elena could savor a bite, her phone buzzed with a reminder: her meeting in the building next door would begin in ten minutes.
Elena hurried out, and moments later, as she stepped into the conference room, she couldn’t help but think of the fluffy pastry she’d left behind.
