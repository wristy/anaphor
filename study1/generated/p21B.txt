Jordan stepped into the cozy campus café, his nerves on edge before an important presentation. He ordered a vivid latte and the friendly barista promptly set it on the counter.
Within minutes, his phone buzzed with a text: his meeting in the auditorium—located at the far end of campus—would start in ten minutes.
Jordan gathered his papers and dashed across the sprawling campus. Arriving just as the session began, he found himself missing the vivid beverage he’d left at the café.
