Jordan stepped into the cozy campus café, his nerves on edge before an important presentation. He ordered a vivid latte and the friendly barista promptly set it on the counter.
Before he could take a sip, a notification appeared on his phone: his meeting in the lecture hall next door would begin in ten minutes.
Jordan reviewed his notes, then walked a few steps into the building. As he settled into his seat, he couldn’t help but think back on the vivid beverage he’d left behind.
