Elena walked into the quaint patisserie in her busy neighborhood, eager for a quick treat. She ordered a fluffy croissant, and the cheerful baker promptly placed it on the counter.
Before she could enjoy a taste, her phone buzzed with news: her meeting in an office tower several miles away was now scheduled to start in two hours.
Taking her time to review her notes, Elena eventually embarked on a long journey through crowded streets and over city bridges. When she finally arrived at the remote building, she couldn’t help but miss the fluffy pastry she’d left at the patisserie.
