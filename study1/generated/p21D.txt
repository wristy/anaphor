Jordan stepped into the cozy campus café, his anxiety building before a crucial presentation. He ordered a vivid latte and the barista efficiently set it on the counter.
Soon, his phone rang: his meeting—now scheduled at a distant conference center several miles away—would begin in two hours.
Taking his time to review his notes, Jordan later embarked on a long walk across campus and through busy streets. When he finally entered the remote center, he couldn’t help but long for the vivid beverage he had left behind at the café.
